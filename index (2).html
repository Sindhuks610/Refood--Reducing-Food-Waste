How It Works

  1. Donors list surplus food items.
  2. Non-profits and individuals browse available food.
  3. Pickup or delivery is arranged directly.
  • Donors:
    1. Login by providing basic essential credentials
    2. provide your Location
    3. Provide an estimated amount of food
    4. You're Ready to Go. We would provide you the Estimated amount of time for the collection of food.
    5. Thank you for Donating.

  • NGO/Non profit organizations:
    1. Login by providing basic essential credentials
    2. Kindly Share the location,number of people and appropriate photos for validation.
    3. Welcome to the family.
    4. We would alert the availability of food.
    5. On request, We would deliver the quality food within some estimated time
    6. Thank You.
Providing quality food is our responsibility; achieving zero hunger is our mission.
